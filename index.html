Orient Productions

Orient for Film and Theatre Productions was created in Cairo in May 2007 as a private production company. Its main mission is to develop quality cultural projects within the fields of performing and visual arts involving artists from Egypt, the Arab world & the West.

D-CAF

Downtown Contemporary Arts Festival (D-CAF) D-CAF breaks new ground by using non-traditional sites such as historical buildings, storefronts, alleyways, and rooftops for performances, events, and art installations, with the aim of bringing both audiences and performers to engage with the city in a new way.

The Falaki Theatre

A contemporary performing arts and music space dedicated to presenting first- rate and professional theatre, music, and dance events. Re-launching in 2012, Falaki Theatre has continued to host a variety of both local and international artists of the highest calibre.

Temple

Strives to create new and relevant Egyptian theatre that is sensitive to the contemporary context in both form and content. Producing several major plays since its inception, Temple has remained significantly active over the past twenty years.

SEE Foundation

Studio Emad Eddin Foundation is a unique project in the Arab World that offers rehearsal, training and residency space to independent troupes and artists in the field of performing arts in Egypt and the Arab World.
